Learn more section


This section contains links to further resources which will allow you to discover further information about forest bathing.

It is likely that your nearest forest may also have published further information about how to best experience their site including travel and safety tips.

Many forests also run guided walks, which may be a good way to start getting involved with the local natural environment.
